About Youtube

YouTube is a video-sharing platform that allows users to upload, share, and view videos. Founded in February 2005 by Chad Hurley, Steve Chen, and Jawed Karim, it quickly gained popularity and was acquired by Google in November 2006. Today, it stands as one of the largest online platforms for video content, featuring a vast array of user-generated and corporate media.
